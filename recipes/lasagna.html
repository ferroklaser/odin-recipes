Lasagna Recipe

Description

This hearty lasagna features layers of tender pasta, rich marinara sauce, creamy ricotta, and a savory blend of ground beef and Italian sausage. Baked to perfection with a golden, bubbling layer of mozzarella and Parmesan, it's a comforting, delicious dish that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions. Simple to make but full of flavor, this lasagna will have everyone coming back for seconds!

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp of white sugar
  • Parsley
  • Dried basil Leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Flennel seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
